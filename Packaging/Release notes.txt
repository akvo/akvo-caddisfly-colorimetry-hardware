Description:
First Iteration of the Packaging for Caddisfly. The kit box is designed to hold:

50 Reagent-filled Capsules
1 Phone with its Backcase attachment
1 Testing Chamber
2 Dilution Syringes
1 Mixing chamber for dilution
1 pack of 50 pH Strips

The Kit is designed to be lasercut from Acrylic and PU foam.
